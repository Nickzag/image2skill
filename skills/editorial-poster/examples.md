# Editorial Poster — Examples

These examples are sanitized. They avoid real logos, private workflow traces, official marks, and third-party brand instructions.

## Coffee grounds recycling booth

```text
Loose white-paper gouache-and-marker editorial poster about a community coffee-grounds recycling booth. Volunteers sort spent grounds into compost buckets, filters, burlap sacks, scales, seedling pots, and handwritten collection tags. Rough cobalt route lines show the recycling loop; big imperfect text “GROUND AGAIN”. No cafe logos, no real brand marks, no unrelated dice or shoes.
```

## River tower photography walk

```text
Loose editorial poster about a public evening photography walk under an abstract twisting observation tower. Ordinary people hold phones and compact cameras, sight lines cross the page, plaza railings and river reflections appear as sketch marks, rough black serif text “LOOK UP”. No real tower logo, no official signage, no brand camera marks.
```
