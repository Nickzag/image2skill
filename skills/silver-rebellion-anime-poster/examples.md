# Examples — Silver Rebellion Anime Poster

## Example 1 — Prototype golden mecha poster

```text
Create a vertical cinematic sci-fi anime theatrical poster featuring a prototype one-eyed cyclops-visor biomechanical giant combat unit in a tight three-quarter upper-body portrait. The machine should feel solemn, restrained, experimental, and mythic, with white and golden-yellow armor, amber hazard accents, a single luminous visor, and a strong sacred-war-machine silhouette.

Design the body as exposed cybernetic anatomy: glossy white segmented armor plates, black mechanical inner structure, gold circuitry, pistons, bolts, cables, scratched lacquered surfaces, chipped edges, and battle-worn metallic details. Use a saturated golden-yellow background with ivory, black, metallic gold, and cold blue accents. Add black ink splatters, grunge marks, diagonal speed lines, paint slashes, sparks, smoke, debris, glowing fragments, and cyan energy streaks.

Typography system: add a huge rough white brush-calligraphy-style English main title across the lower third reading "EVA UNIT 00", with yellow and black paint strokes behind it. Other tiny supporting typography may include fictional Chinese/Japanese-inspired labels, English code strings, ritual triangles, and angelic circuit sigils. All typography must be decorative fictional graphic design, not copied from any real title, brand, franchise, studio, creator, or logo. Add exactly one small integrated handwritten signature reading "NickZag" in dark-gold ink on the lower brush stroke, unobtrusive but clear. Aspect ratio: vertical poster, 2:3.
```
