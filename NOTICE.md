# Notice

This repository contains prompt-writing skills and documentation for educational, research, and non-commercial prompt-engineering use.

## Third-party rights

The skills are written as generic visual systems. They should not be used to reproduce third-party brand logos, trademark lockups, studio marks, official product packaging, or copyrighted promotional graphics.

If a user applies a skill to a public character, brand, fictional universe, or recognizable property, that user is responsible for respecting applicable rights, platform rules, and local law. Third-party characters, trademarks, brands, logos, and original intellectual property remain with their respective owners.

## No logo reproduction

Do not include prompts such as:

- "place the official logo";
- "recreate the exact brand mark";
- "copy the studio watermark";
- "use the real team/company emblem";
- "make it look like official merchandise packaging".

Use generic replacements instead:

- "fictional small patch";
- "abstract emblem";
- "invented warning label";
- "non-branded technical sticker";
- "unreadable graphic marks".

## Public examples

Examples in this repository are sanitized. They avoid private workflow traces and avoid instructions to reproduce third-party logos or marks.
